chicken

Let me start off by saying that putting KFC at the bottom spot isn't meant to be negative. KFC is like the McDonald's of fried chicken. They do a decent job on the quality, and they keep the flavor train rolling with Nashville Hot, Georgia Gold, and whatever other fried chicken trends they're monitoring. However, every other single chain on this list was designed to be better than the finger lickin' good poultry you can get from KFC. That being said, KFC definitely deserves the fried chicken love 'cause they still make some pretty damn good chicken.
